Welcome. My name is Magnolia, and I'm a fourth year Design and Psychology double-major at the University of California, Davis. If you are reading this, you've located my unofficial, "official" portfolio, containing some of my personal work that I've completed entirely outside of the classroom setting. While classwork tends to demonstrate one's ability to follow rules and implement learned skills, I wanted to focus on pieces that truly reflected me, my interests, and my independent, creative capabilities. As a result, my portfolio contains several hand-drawn portraits--created with analog tools and hours worth of patience. My affinity for things hand-drawn is driven by a desire to express myself in (what I believe to be) the rawest form. If you see anything that interests or resignates with you, please do not hesitate to contact me. I am willing to create with or for all who would like to make this world a little more beautiful.

Many thanks.
